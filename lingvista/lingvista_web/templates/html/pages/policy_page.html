Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 12.04.2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Lingvista. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal data when you use our Russian language learning platform.

By accessing or using Lingvista, you agree to this policy. If you disagree, please do not use our services.

2. Information We Collect

A. Personal Information You Provide

  • Account Data: Name, email, username, password (for registration)
  • Learning Progress: Test results, completed lessons

B. Automatically Collected Data

  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device info, pages visited
  • Cookies & Tracking: Session cookies (for login), analytics (Google Analytics), and preference cookies

3. How We Use Your Data

We process your information for:

  • Providing Services: Account management, lesson progress tracking
  • Improving Our Platform: Analyzing user behavior to enhance content
  • Security: Preventing fraud and unauthorized access
  • Legal Compliance: Meeting GDPR, CCPA, or other regulations

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Under GDPR, we rely on:

  • Contractual necessity (to provide our services)
  • Your consent (for marketing emails or cookies)
  • Legitimate interests (improving our website, security)

You may withdraw consent anytime (e.g., unsubscribe from emails).

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We only share data with:

  • Service Providers: Hosting (AWS, Heroku), analytics (Google)
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law (e.g., court orders)
  • Business Transfers: If our platform is acquired

We do not sell your data to advertisers.

6. International Data Transfers

If you are in the EU, your data may be transferred outside the European Economic Area (EEA) to countries with adequate protections (e.g., US providers under GDPR Standard Contractual Clauses).

7. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use:

  • Essential Cookies: For site functionality (e.g., login sessions)
  • Analytics Cookies: To analyze traffic (opt-out via browser settings)
  • Marketing Cookies: Only with your consent

Manage preferences via our Cookie Banner or browser settings.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data:

  • As long as your account is active
  • For legal/compliance reasons (e.g., transaction records)
  • Anonymized data may be kept for analytics

9. Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

You may:

  • Access or download your data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account/data (unless legally required to keep it)
  • Object to processing (e.g., marketing emails)
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (EU users)

10. Children's Privacy

Our service is not for children under 13 (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect their data.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with a new "Last Updated" date. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
